Who we are

About Us

Founded in 1996 by Bryan Rafanelli, Rafanelli Events has evolved into a beacon of innovation and excellence in the event planning industry. Our full-service approach and meticulous attention to detail have earned us a stellar reputation, with offices strategically located in New York City, Palm Beach, and Boston, extending our reach globally.

At Rafanelli Events, we specialize in a broad spectrum of gatherings, from destination weddings and large-scale corporate events to star-studded galas and impactful non-profit fundraisers. Each event, whether hosting 50 or 15,000 attendees, is treated as a unique creative challenge, deserving of a custom-tailored celebration.

Our team of fifty dedicated design and production professionals embraces the philosophy that we’re not just planning events—we’re crafting extraordinary experiences. This commitment drives us to consistently deliver events that are not only visually stunning but also deeply personal, reflecting the unique tastes and personalities of each client.
